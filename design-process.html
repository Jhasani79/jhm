UX DESIGN PROCESS

Making a website is an enjoyable and straightforward process, but requires certain skills.
The process I use is very straightforward. There are six steps to the process.

Before the discovery, there will be a first call, where I determine if we are a good fit.

After the discovery, which includes a series of questions, a proposal will be given, and the deal will be closed.

There will be quite some feedback required to deliver the right product for you, but once that has been clarified the wire framing will be done.

Once that has been cleared the visual design, and the development can begin.

Everything will be delivered per our proposed timeline.
